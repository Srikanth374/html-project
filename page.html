ABOUT RDEA


RDEA 2021 is a conceptualization of Summentor Pro Sales & Marketing Consultants, in association with ANUDIP Foundation, to provide real success stories a platform to reach more people at a National level and unlock the potential offered by rural regions, meet with a wide range of innovations under economic, environmental ,and social segments. Such stories can inspire many to replicate similar initiatives across India, to effectively raise awareness towards the long term vision for Rural Areas developed by the Government of India.
