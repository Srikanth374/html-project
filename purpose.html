APPLICATION PROCESS


SUBMIT YOUR NOMINATION

Register your interest at RDEA 2021. Fill in the Detail in the nomination form Select the best suitable category to apply. Pay the selection process fee of Rs 7,000 + 18% GST per category. Submit your project details in PPT/PDF/WALKTHROUGH/

SHORTLISTING AND JUDGING

All nominations are reviewed by the shortlist committee and five candidates per category will be shortlisted to win at the RDEA 2021 NOMINATION. Winners will also be invited to create short videos after the results are declared.

AWARDS CEREMONY

The award winners will be announced on a weekly basis starting February 10th 2021.

CRITERIA FOR SELECTION


Individual Awards:

Organizational Category:

Each entry should be accompanied by a write-up in a maximum of 15 pages, Profile of the company & Brochure; Media write-ups & interviews as annexure can be further attached along with relevant photographs.
